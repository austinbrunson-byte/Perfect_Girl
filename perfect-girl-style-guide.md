# Perfect Girl — prompt generation brief

Paste or upload this whole file into a fresh chat and ask the model to generate more
prompts. It contains the locked-in voice, the full set of approved examples, the
rejected examples, and the exact output format to return.

---

## What this is

A yes/no game. Each card shows a photo of a "perfect girl" and one dealbreaker,
phrased as **"Perfect girl, but… <prompt>"**. The player decides if they would
still say yes. Every prompt you write is the text that follows "Perfect girl, but…",
so it must read naturally after that lead-in (start lowercase, end with a period).

## The voice (strict)

- **Deadpan.** Flat, specific, stated as plain fact. No jokes, no punchline, no wink,
  nothing that announces itself as funny. The humor comes only from the content
  being absurd while the delivery stays completely straight.
- **One clean sentence.** Occasionally two short ones, but one is the default and
  almost always better. If a second sentence only explains or embellishes, cut it.
- **A recurring trait, never a one-time event.** It is something true of her /
  the relationship in general — not a single incident. ("every morning", "3% of
  the time", "at all times").
- **Tie it to her or to the two of you.** It is about who she is or what being with
  her is like — not a free-floating fact about the world.
- **Name the roach plainly** — "a roach", "roach legs", "roach antenna". Never cute
  euphemisms ("something with mass", "a third occupant", "something that closes
  back"). Say the roach.
- **Use a plausible percentage or rate** for the roach items where it fits. The
  favorite pattern is: **"X% of the time [ordinary shared moment], a roach [appears]."**

## The mix

Roughly **half roach, half ordinary**, plus a light sprinkle of two running bits:

- **Roach (~50%):** the girl and cockroaches are quietly, permanently intertwined.
- **Ordinary human dealbreakers (~40%):** real, relatable relationship red flags —
  habits, exes, money, family, manners, punctuality, control.
- **Shaq bit (a few):** she is intermittently/partly Shaquille O'Neal — size,
  strength, free-throw failure. Refer to him only as **"Shaq"**, never the full name.
- **Insurance bit (a few):** she is intermittently **The General** / an insurance
  spokesperson — premiums, deductibles, coupons, quotes.
